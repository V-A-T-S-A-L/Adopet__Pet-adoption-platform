Who Are We?

Adopet was founded in 2024 by a group of animal lovers who recognized the need for a more effective and compassionate approach to pet adoption. What began as a small local initiative has since grown into a trusted platform, connecting pets in need with families across the country. Our journey has been fueled by a deep passion for animal welfare and a commitment to making a positive impact on the lives of both pets and people. Over the years, we have helped thousands of pets find their forever homes by working closely with shelters, rescue groups, and volunteers.

This is a non-profitable organisation and our only aim is to provide shelters for animals.
